Sterling Wiki is a sterling's enterprise digital encyclopedia that gives you Information you need, when you need it. It contains up to date information about anything and everything Sterling! from business procedures, sterling products/services, staff Information, units of the bank, excos, guidelines, policies and a whole lot more.



Sterling Wiki is an open-collaborative digital encyclopedia for all things Sterling! Created through the collaborative effort of all sterling employees. The platform gives anyone with an activite sterling AD credentials access through a one time registeration. Once you are sigined into the platform, you can search for any sterling related topic that is relevant to you and your business process and also you create multiple topics that you know will be rellevant to others, as well as contribute to existing topics.
